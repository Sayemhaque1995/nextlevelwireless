About Next Level Wireless

Your trusted local experts for device repair, specializing in fast turnarounds and complex fixes.

Expert Repairs, Friendly Service

At Next Level Wireless, we're passionate about technology and dedicated to providing top-tier repair services with exceptional customer care. Located conveniently in Philadelphia, our team of certified technicians brings years of hands-on experience fixing a wide array of electronic devices.

We specialize in repairs for iPhones, iPads, Samsung Galaxy devices, MacBooks, Windows PCs, gaming consoles, and more. Our expertise extends to intricate micro-soldering and logic board repairs, tackling issues that other shops might deem unfixable.

We use only high-quality replacement parts, ensuring durability and performance, backed by our 90-day warranty. Need accessories or phone activation? We've got you covered there too.

Please Note: While we repair a wide range of electronics, we currently do not purchase used devices from customers.
